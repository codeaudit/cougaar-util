Cougaar 9.0 Repackaging Details

Package Cheat Sheet (new packages are in bold)

org.cougaar.core.cluster
in general split into:  org.cougaar.core.agent & org.cougaar.core.blackboard

org.cougaar.core.society
in general split into:  org.cougaar.core.node & org.cougaar.core.mts

org.cougaar.domain no longer exits
org.cougaar.domain.mlm. and org.cougaar.domain.glm become:
org.cougaar.mlm and org.cougaar.glm

Domain, Factory, Registry, RootDomain, etc. can be found in
org.cougaar.core.domain

All Service APIs are in:
org.cougaar.core.service

All Service impls are in their components packages... for example:
org.cougaar.core.agent.service has
alarm.*
domain.*
registry.*
uid.*
etc
